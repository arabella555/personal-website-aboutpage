about me

Hi! I’m Arabella, a second-year student at the University of Waterloo, studying Global Business and Digital Arts. I’m passionate about using digital media and design to tell compelling stories that captivate and engage audiences. When I’m not immersed in my creative projects, you can find me café hopping downtown or re-watching the Scream franchise!

MY BIG THREE

01. Design with Purpose

I always consider the emotional impact of everything I create on audiences and users, ensuring that each design effectively resonates and communicates its intended message.

02. Balance Creativity and Functionality

In my designs, I strive to create a balance between aesthetic appeal and practical usability.

03. Stay Current and Adapt

Design tools and trends constantly evolve. I ensure to stay updated on industry trends, adapting my skills and techniques accordingly.
